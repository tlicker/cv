Tolja Spesyvyi

Postdoctoral Fellow at Heyrovsky Institute.

My research lies in the field of gas phase ion chemistry, ion transport in gases and mass spectrometry. Besides fundamental phenomena I am interested in the instrumental implementations of techniques for RF ion guides, cluster filtering by means of their shape and state using ion mobility, complex metal-organic clusters generation using electrospray ion source and clusters doping (association ion-molecule reactions) in vacuum.
